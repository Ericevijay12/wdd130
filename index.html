Nigeria

Nigeria is located in West Africa and is often referred to as the "Giant of Africa" due to its large population and economy. With over 200 million people, it's the most populous country in Africa and one of the most ethnically diverse nations in the world. Nigeria has a rich cultural heritage, beautiful landscapes ranging from rainforests to savannas, and is known for its vibrant music, film industry (Nollywood), and cuisine. The country gained independence from British colonial rule in 1960 and has since developed into a major economic power in Africa, largely due to its oil production.
