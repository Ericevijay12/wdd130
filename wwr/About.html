History

Founded in 1995, Rafting Company has been a pioneer in white water rafting adventures. Our journey began with a small team of passionate river enthusiasts who believed in the thrill of conquering rapids and the beauty of untouched landscapes.

Over the years, we've expanded our operations, explored new rivers, and welcomed adventurers from all walks of life. From the roaring currents of the Colorado River to the serene beauty of the Snake River, we've crafted unforgettable experiences for our clients.

Our commitment to safety, environmental stewardship, and exceptional customer service remains unwavering. Join us as we continue to write new chapters in the history of white water rafting!
