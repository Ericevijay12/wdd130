Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this rafting site is to provide an online presence for our rafting business, allowing potential customers to learn about our services, book trips, and get excited about white water rafting adventures.

Audience

The target audience for the rafting website includes:

  • Adventure enthusiasts looking for thrilling outdoor activities.
  • Families seeking exciting and safe vacation activities.
  • Corporate groups interested in team-building events.
Scenario questions:
  1. Who are the primary users of the website? Adventure enthusiasts, families, and corporate groups.
  2. What information are users looking for? Details about rafting trips, booking information, safety measures, and contact information.
  3. Why would users choose our rafting services? For the adventure, safety, and excellent customer service we provide.
