Featured Adventures

Family Rapids Adventure

Easy - Class I-II

Perfect for families and first-time rafters! Enjoy gentle rapids, stunning scenery, and wildlife spotting along our most peaceful river section. This half-day adventure includes professional guides, safety equipment, and riverside snacks.

  • Duration: 4 hours
  • Age Requirement: 6+ years
  • Group Size: 6-8 people
  • Season: May - September
  • Price: $85 per person

Big Mallard Rapids

Moderate - Class III-IV

Experience the perfect balance of excitement and safety! Navigate through challenging rapids, deep pools, and spectacular rock formations. This full-day adventure includes lunch and professional photography service.

  • Duration: 8 hours
  • Age Requirement: 12+ years
  • Group Size: 6-10 people
  • Season: April - October
  • Price: $145 per person

Los Dientes del Diablo

Extreme - Class V

The ultimate adrenaline rush! Conquer the most challenging rapids in the region. This expert-level adventure features massive waves, technical maneuvers, and breathtaking drops. Only for experienced rafters seeking the ultimate challenge.

  • Duration: 10 hours
  • Age Requirement: 18+ years
  • Group Size: 4-6 people
  • Season: June - August
  • Price: $295 per person

Seasonal River Conditions

Our rivers offer different experiences throughout the year, with varying water levels and conditions that affect the intensity of your adventure.

Spring (Mar-May)

High water levels, cooler temperatures, most challenging conditions

Summer (Jun-Aug)

Perfect conditions, warm weather, ideal for all skill levels

Fall (Sep-Nov)

Lower water levels, beautiful scenery, great for beginners

Winter (Dec-Feb)

Limited trips, indoor training programs available
